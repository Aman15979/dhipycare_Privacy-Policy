Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains in comprehensive detail how DhipyCare (“Company”, “we”, “our”, “us”) manages personal, medical, and technical data when users interact with our services through our website, mobile application, or communication platforms. The purpose of this policy is to provide transparency regarding data handling practices and to empower users with knowledge and control over their information. By accessing and using DhipyCare, you consent to the collection, storage, and use of your information as described herein. If you do not agree with this policy, you are advised to discontinue use of our services. This Policy applies regardless of the country from which the user accesses our platform but is primarily governed by India’s Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP Act 2023).

1.Scope of This Policy

1.1 This Privacy Policy applies to the DhipyCare website, mobile applications, and any digital service operated by the Company. 1.2 This policy governs the handling of personal, medical, professional, and technical information submitted by users, nurses, and doctors. 1.3 The policy applies to data collected through forms, authentication, onboarding, profile creation, and booking procedures. 1.4 By using the platform, users consent to data processing as outlined here. 1.5 The policy applies to data voluntarily submitted and automatically collected through applications. 1.6 Data collected offline or manually by email, call, or communication falls within this policy’s scope. 1.7 DhipyCare may expand this policy if features or services evolve. 1.8 This policy is effective across India and applies globally where users access our services. 1.9 This Privacy Policy does not govern websites or third-party pages linked outside DhipyCare. 1.10 In case of conflict between this Policy and Terms & Conditions, this Policy governs personal data usage.

2.Medical Disclaimer

2.1 DhipyCare provides digital access to healthcare-related services including doctor consultations, nursing assistance, lab tests, and home healthcare coordination. 2.2 DhipyCare serves as a digital facilitator and not as the direct provider of clinical treatment or emergency care. 2.3 All medical recommendations and consultation details are provided exclusively by the respective healthcare professionals or service providers. 2.4 DhipyCare does not guarantee treatment outcomes or symptom recovery timelines. 2.5 Online consultations may have limitations compared to physical medical examinations. 2.6 Users must not rely solely on digital communication for critical medical decisions. 2.7 DhipyCare is not responsible for service interruptions caused by user internet connectivity or device failure. 2.8 DhipyCare does not operate as a hospital, emergency unit, or ambulance service. 2.9 In cases of medical emergency, users must contact the nearest hospital or emergency service immediately. 2.10 By using the platform, users acknowledge that independent professional judgment belongs strictly to the healthcare provider and not to DhipyCare.

3.Information We Collect

3.1 We collect personal information including Name, Email, Phone Number, and Residential Address for account creation and service access. 3.2 We may collect Age, Gender, or Date of Birth when entered for service eligibility. 3.3 We collect health-related information voluntarily submitted by users for consultation or service booking. 3.4 For nurse onboarding, we may collect License Number, Experience, Qualification, and Professional background details. 3.5 For doctor onboarding, we collect Specialization, Medical License Number, Qualifications, Clinic Address, and Work Experience. 3.6 We collect uploaded documents including Professional Certificates, Government ID Proofs, and Profile Photos for verification. 3.7 We collect device identifiers and analytics such as device model and operating system for security and performance. 3.8 We may collect approximate or precise location for navigation, booking fulfillment, and service availability. 3.9 We collect verification data through OTP and optional Google login authentication. 3.10 Any additional optional information submitted during communication or profile updates is stored as part of user records.

4.How We Use the Information

4.1 Information is used for registration, account authentication, and identity verification. 4.2 Personal and medical details are used to assign appropriate healthcare professionals. 4.3 Information is used for booking management, confirmations, and service updates. 4.4 Platform optimization uses technical and analytical data. 4.5 Communication data allows us to send notifications, reminders, and alerts. 4.6 Information is used for support channels and resolving queries. 4.7 We use data for fraud prevention, misuse, and security compliance. 4.8 Aggregated non-personal data may be used for improving service performance. 4.9 Data may support lawful audit, investigation, or regulatory reporting. 4.10 Users grant consent to process data for accessing DhipyCare’s services.

5.Medical & Health Information Confidentiality

5.1 DhipyCare classifies medical information as sensitive and confidential. 5.2 Access to health information is granted only to authorized professionals. 5.3 Medical details are used solely for consultation or service delivery. 5.4 Medical data is stored with secure infrastructure. 5.5 We follow professional ethics of patient confidentiality. 5.6 Medical information is not shared externally without consent or legal requirement. 5.7 Users may request deletion of their medical information. 5.8 DhipyCare does not commercially benefit from medical data. 5.9 Sensitive information is processed only for legitimate healthcare operations. 5.10 Data retention follows legal, regulatory, and operational requirements.

6.Background Location & Tracking

6.1 The Nurse App collects precise location to support navigation and visit verification. 6.2 Location is collected when the app is open or minimized for operational continuity. 6.3 Tracking occurs only during assigned service tasks. 6.4 The app does not collect location after being manually closed. 6.5 Location tracking ensures accuracy and safety during home visits. 6.6 Disabling location may affect service delivery functionality. 6.7 Background location is stored securely for compliance purposes. 6.8 Tracking data is not used for unrelated profiling. 6.9 Location data supports operational analysis and process improvement. 6.10 Location tracking aligns with Google Play and service compliance requirements.

7.Data Sharing & Third Parties

7.1 DhipyCare may share essential information with healthcare professionals delivering services. 7.2 We use Firebase for authentication and secure cloud storage. 7.3 We use Firestore for managing user data, professional profiles, and booking records. 7.4 We use Firebase Storage for storing documents and verification files. 7.5 Google Maps API supports navigation and service location mapping. 7.6 Google reCAPTCHA collects device interaction data for fraud prevention. 7.7 Data is shared strictly with aligned service providers. 7.8 We do not sell user data to advertisers. 7.9 DhipyCare does not share personal data with unauthorized third parties. 7.10 Admin dashboard (in current version) does not collect or process user data.

8.Data Storage, Security & Retention

8.1 Data is stored securely using encrypted servers. 8.2 Access control prevents unauthorized handling. 8.3 Retention is limited to compliance requirements. 8.4 Users may delete their accounts independently. 8.5 Locally stored data is removed upon app deletion. 8.6 We protect data against loss and breach. 8.7 Backup storage may temporarily retain deleted information. 8.8 Data is not retained beyond lawful need. 8.9 Users acknowledge inherent risks of internet-based systems. 8.10 DhipyCare reviews security systems regularly.
