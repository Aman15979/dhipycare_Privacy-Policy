Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains in comprehensive detail how DhipyCare (“Company”, “we”, “our”, “us”) manages personal, medical, and technical data when users interact with our services through our website, mobile application, or communication platforms. The purpose of this policy is to provide transparency regarding data handling practices and to empower users with knowledge and control over their information. By accessing and using DhipyCare, you consent to the collection, storage, and use of your information as described herein. If you do not agree with this policy, you are advised to discontinue use of our services. This Policy applies regardless of the country from which the user accesses our platform but is primarily governed by India’s Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP Act 2023).

1.Scope of This Policy

1.1 This Privacy Policy applies to all personal and medical information collected through the DhipyCare website, mobile application, and associated digital services operated under the DhipyCare brand. 1.2 This Policy governs both online and offline data collection initiated by DhipyCare for the purposes of service delivery, consultation, support, or legal compliance. 1.3 The Policy governs the collection of data from users, patients, healthcare professionals, and service suppliers who interact with the DhipyCare ecosystem. 1.4 This Policy applies to data collected through forms, bookings, chat support, notifications, integrations, digital reports, and analytical tools. 1.5 The terms of this Policy reflect mandatory requirements under India’s DPDP Act, 2023, which governs personal data usage. 1.6 DhipyCare may extend compliance in the future to meet global standards where required by international operations. 1.7 The Policy applies to data whether collected directly from the user, indirectly through service usage, or through automated systems. 1.8 This Policy does not apply to third-party websites that may be accessible through links from our platform. 1.9 Users are encouraged to review third-party privacy terms when interacting outside DhipyCare services. 1.10 If there is a conflict between this Policy and any term displayed elsewhere, this Privacy Policy shall prevail concerning data handling.
